## Purlins

Our high-strength C- and Z-shaped Purlins are lightweight and durable, allowing for flexible design and quick installation. They provide reliable structural support for roofing and wall cladding and are made of superior-grade steel. These purlins are ideal for use in industrial and commercial buildings because they are resistant to corrosion and can withstand heavy loads.
