## MS Plate / Channel / Angle

**MS Plate**, **MS Channel**, and **MS Angle**– These all are Mild Steel (MS) structural products commonly used in construction, fabrication, and manufacturing.

## MS Plate

- **Flat sheet** of mild steel.
- Used in: construction, fabrication, tanks, machinery bases.
- Thickness: 2mm–100mm+

## MS Channel

- **C-shaped** steel beam.
- Used in: building frames, trailers, support structures.
- Size: starts from 75×40 mm (standard length: 6m–12m)

## MS Angle

- **L-shaped** steel bar.
- Used in: frames, racks, towers, supports.
- Comes in equal/unequal sizes (25×25 mm to 150×150 mm)
