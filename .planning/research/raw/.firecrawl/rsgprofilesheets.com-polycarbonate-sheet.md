## Polycarbonate Sheet

RSG offers polycarbonate sheets having good light transmittance, good impact resistance, and UV protection. The sheets are most suited for skylights, greenhouses, and industrial sheds, combining strength with flexibility and weather durability. With a lightweight nature and easy installation, our sheets promise clarity and performance even under the most extreme weather conditions.

Polycarbonate sheets are transparent or translucent thermoplastic sheets known for their impact resistance, UV

protection, and lightweight nature. Often used as an alternative to glass or acrylic in roofing and daylighting

applications.

**Types:**

• **Solid Polycarbonate Sheets–** Looks like glass, more impact-resistant.

• **Multiwall Sheets–** Hollow structure for better insulation.

**•Corrugated Polycarbonate Sheets–** For roofing and sheds.

**Uses:**

•Skylights, canopies, greenhouses, car sheds.

•Roofing for walkways, stadiums, commercial buildings.

•Used in industrial sheds and roofing systems for natural lighting.

**Key Features:**

•High impact resistance (almost unbreakable).

•UV protection coating.

•Lightweight and flexible
