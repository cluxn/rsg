## Decking Sheet

Decking Sheets were designed and developed to provide a strong and durable structural base for floors or roofs. They are made of galvanized steel, which has a high tensile strength and load-bearing capacity. These sheets allow for faster and more consistent construction timelines, making them ideal for multi-story buildings and industrial constructions.

## Decking Sheet

Decking sheets are structural steel sheets used in flooring systems, roofing systems, or as a base for concrete slabs in
buildings. They are high-tensile galvanized sheets formed into a corrugated profile.

![](https://rsgprofilesheets.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/WhatsApp-Image-2025-07-24-at-4.17.33-AM-300x152.jpeg)

## Uses

- Used as formwork for concrete slabs in multi-storey buildings.
- Common in industrial structures, warehouses, and commercial construction.
- Also used for roof decking in pre-engineered buildings.

## Key Features

- High strength and load-bearing capacity.
- Reduces concrete usage due to structural design.
- Fast and easy installation.
- Can be used as a permanent framework.
- Corrosion-resistant (when galvanized).
