What are spice oils?

Spice oils are concentrated extracts obtained from spices like clove, black pepper, cinnamon, cardamom, etc.

Are your spice oils natural?

Yes, our spice oils are primarily natural extracts, and we clearly mention if any product is rectified or blended.

Where can spice oils be used?

They are used in flavours, pharma, oral care, seasoning, aromatherapy, and fragrance applications, depending on the grade.

Can spice oils be used in food?

Food-grade spice oils can be used in F&B, but always at low, controlled dosages as per guidelines.

Do you ship spice oils internationally?

Yes, we export spice oils globally with proper documentation and packing.
