## Colour Coated Roofing Sheet

![](https://rsgprofilesheets.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/07/WhatsApp-Image-2025-07-24-at-4.27.54-AM-300x300.jpeg)

Color coated roofing sheets are galvanized or galvalume metal sheets that are coated with a layer of protective paint or polymer coating, giving them color, shine, and corrosion resistance. These are mainly used for roofing and cladding purposes.

**Materials Used:**

Galvanized Iron (GI)

Galvalume Steel

Aluminium-Zinc Alloy Steel

Pre-Painted Galvanized Iron (PPGI)

Pre-Painted Galvalume Iron (PPGL)
