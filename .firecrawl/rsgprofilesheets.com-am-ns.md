## About AM/NS

AM/NS India, a joint venture between ArcelorMittal and Nippon Steel – two of the world’s leading steel companies – is an integrated flat carbon steel manufacturer – from iron ore to ready-to-market products. The company’s manufacturing facilities comprise ironmaking, steelmaking and downstream facilities spread across India.

## Advantages of AM/NS

AM/NS roofing sheets offer a range of advantages, including exceptional durability, corrosion resistance, and lightweight construction, ensuring long-lasting performance with reduced structural load. Their reflective surfaces provide thermal efficiency, keeping interiors cooler and saving energy, while their modern designs and color options enhance aesthetic appeal. These sheets also boast impact and fire resistance, making them ideal for harsh weather and safety-conscious applications. With customizable options in thickness, lengths, and profiles, AM/NS roofing sheets are a versatile and reliable solution for diverse roofing needs.
