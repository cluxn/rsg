## About Jindal Sabrang

Steel has long been synonymous with strength and durability in construction and fabrication, yet it has rarely been associated with vibrancy or colorfulness. Jindal presents Jindal Sabrang, the latest innovation from Jindal India, designed to inject add a splash of color into the traditionally colorless world of steel. Derived from the Sanskrit word for “rainbow of colors,” Sabrang aims to not only enhance the visual appeal of steel but also extend the lifespan of structures by providing an additional layer of Zinc/Alu-Zinc offering corrosion resistance.

## 1220MM sheet Specifications

These sheets are commonly used in the construction industry for roofing and cladding purposes, offering durable protection against weather conditions. They come in various materials such as metal, polycarbonate, and fiber cement, each serving different purposes and preferences.

## 1440MM sheet Specifications

A **1440mm roofing sheet** refers to a roofing material with a width of 1440 millimeters (1.44 meters). These sheets are widely used in various construction projects, from residential homes to commercial and industrial buildings. The increased width compared to other standard sheets allows for greater coverage, reducing the number of sheets required and simplifying installation. Like 1220mm sheets, 1440mm roofing sheets come in several materials, including metal, polycarbonate, and fiber cement, each with specific benefits.
