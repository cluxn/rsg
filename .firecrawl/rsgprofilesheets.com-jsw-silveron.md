## About JSW Silveron+

JSW Silveron+ is a premium category roofing sheet coated with alloy substrates, which contain Aluminum, Zinc,

and Silicon. Equipped with Anti-Corrosion technology, It prevents early corrosion of steel and substantially

increases the life of the product.

The substrates are applied with the hot-dipping process, which has proven superior resistance to corrosion. This

makes it more effective and lasting than traditionally used Zinc coated steel sheets in a similar environment.

Its lightweight appearance, excellent corrosion resistance, good heat-reflectivity, and protection from rupture

make it an effective solution for roofing & wall profiles for residential, commercial, and industrial projects.
