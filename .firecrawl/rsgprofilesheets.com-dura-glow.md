## Advantages of DURA GLOW

DuraGlow Roofing Sheets offer strong durability, excellent weather resistance, and long-lasting colour protection. They are lightweight, easy to install, and designed to resist rust, fading, and leakage. With a smooth finish and sturdy profile, DuraGlow provides reliable performance, low maintenance, and an attractive look for any building.

DuraGlow Roofing Sheets are engineered using high-tensile steel substrates and advanced colour-coating technology to deliver superior structural performance. The sheets feature a precision-formed profile that enhances load-bearing capability and improves wind uplift resistance. Their multi-layer protective coating system provides exceptional defence against oxidation, chemical exposure, and UV degradation, ensuring long-term surface integrity. The optimized sheet geometry supports efficient water runoff, reducing the risk of seepage and thermal expansion stress. With excellent dimensional accuracy, high corrosion resistance, and stable mechanical properties, DuraGlow Roofing Sheets offer a reliable and technically robust solution for industrial, commercial, and large-span roofing applications

## Usage of DURA GLOW

DuraGlow Roofing Sheets are widely used across residential, commercial, and industrial sectors due to their strength, durability, and long-lasting finish. They are ideal for roofing applications in houses, sheds, warehouses, factories, workshops, farm buildings, and parking shelters. In commercial projects, DuraGlow sheets are used for malls, showrooms, office buildings, and canopy structures. Additionally, they are preferred for cladding, boundary wall panels, and protective coverings where weather resistance and neat appearance are required.
