## Galvanized Plain Sheets

GP Sheets are cold-rolled mild steel sheets that are coated with zinc through a process called galvanization to protect the sheet from corrosion and rust. These sheets are plain

and flat (not corrugated)

**Uses / Applications:**

•Automobile body parts (car roofs, trunks, etc.).

•Electrical panels and enclosures.

•Ducting and ventilation systems.

•Fabrication of furniture, racks, and cabinets.

**Key Features:**

•Zinc coating prevents rust and enhances life.

•Smooth surface finish and uniform thickness.

•Good weldability and formability.
